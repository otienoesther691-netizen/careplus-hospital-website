ABOUT CAREPLUS MEDICAL CENTRE

Providing trusted,compassionate,and affordable healthcare to families since 2015. Our committment is to improve lives through excellent medical care and modern healthcare solutions.

OUR STORY

CarePlus medical centre was started in 2016 by Dr Erlin Dreadson from England, who was passionate about improving healthcare in the city of Atlanta. During a Typhoid and Malaria outbreak,the clinic provided lifesaving treatment to the local residents.The clinic was later expanded into a fully equiped medical centre offering a wide range of healthcare services
